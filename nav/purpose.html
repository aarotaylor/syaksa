The goal of Isydia (ih-city-uh) is to deliver deep, intricate, narrative driven sci-fi music. Immerse yourself in beautifully cold soundscapes layered with mysterious melodic lines and disjoint percussive samples. Each track tells a story, what will you hear? Isydia takes inspiration from a wide variety of sources and artists, but most notably from Captain Panic! and Systek's "Monolith" album, and the Dune Saga by Frank Herbert
